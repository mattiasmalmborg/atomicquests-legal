How it works

Each day you receive a set of quests across six categories: Dopamine, Serotonin, Oxytocin, Endorphin, Cortisol, and Testosterone. These are gamification themes inspired by concepts from habit science — not medical claims. Complete quests to earn XP, maintain streaks, and unlock achievements.

AtomicQuests uses neurotransmitter names as gamification themes to make habit-building fun. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and does not claim to affect actual neurochemical levels. It is not a substitute for professional medical care.
