Delete Your Account

Last updated: February 2026

You can delete your AtomicQuests account and all associated data at any time. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete in the App (Recommended)

Follow these steps in AtomicQuests:
  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab
  2. Tap Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Type DELETE to confirm
  5. Tap Delete permanently

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately. You will be signed out automatically.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the in-app deletion flow (e.g. you no longer have access to the app), send a deletion request to:

privacy@atomicquests.app

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • All quest completions and XP history
  • Habits and habit completion logs
  • Challenges and challenge progress
  • Streak data and substance levels
  • Daily mood logs
  • Achievements and rewards
  • Friend connections and social data
  • Activity feed entries
  • All local data on the device

Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All your progress, streaks, levels, and history will be erased and cannot be recovered.

Retention After Deletion

Once deleted, your data is removed from our servers within 30 days. Anonymised aggregate statistics (no personal identifiers) may be retained for service improvement.
